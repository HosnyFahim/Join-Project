How to use this Kanban Board

What is Kanban?

Kanban comes from the Japanese and means signal card.
It is an agile project management method with the use of a classic to-do list.
With the help of Kanban, the workflow in projects can be planned, monitored and controlled more optimally.
The Kanban-Board represents thereby tasks, responsibilities and expirations within a project clearly.
The board is divided into columns with entries for tasks that you can work through in sequence with your project colleagues or employees.

How can I add a Task to the Backlog?

1. Click on the "Add Task" Section
2. There you can give the Task amoung others a title, dircription, category and assign it to a user
3. After you have filled in everything, you can create the Task by clicking the "create task"-button

How can I get the Task on my Board?

1. Each Task created is listed in the Backlog
2. By clicking on the Task in the Backlog you want to add to your Board, you first get an overview over the Task
3. In the overview you than can click a button to add the Task to your Board

How can I change the status of a Task?

1. On your Board, a Task can have one of four statuses (to do, in progress, testing and done)
2. To change the status of a Task, simply click and hold on the Task and drop it into the desired column
